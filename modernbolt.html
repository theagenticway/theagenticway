Sun-Washed Ochre Linen

$18.00 / yard

The Story

This linen blend feels like a warm afternoon. It's breathable with a soft, lived-in texture that only gets better with every wash. The rich ochre hue is incredibly versatile, adding a touch of earthy elegance to any project.

Perfect For: Wide-leg trousers, flowy midi dresses, structured blouses, and artisan throw pillows.

  • Content: 55% Linen / 45% Rayon
  • Width: 54" / 137 cm
  • Weight: 5.9 oz / 200 GSM
  • Designer: The Modern Bolt Exclusives
  • Care: Machine wash cold, tumble dry low.

We believe fabric is more than just material.

It’s the beginning of a story. A story about a dress worn on a perfect summer day, a quilt that becomes a family heirloom, or a set of pillows that finally make a house feel like a home.

The Modern Bolt was founded in 2025 with a simple mission: to provide makers with a curated selection of contemporary, high-quality fabrics that inspire creativity and stand the test of time. We meticulously source our textiles from designers and mills who share our passion for quality and design.
